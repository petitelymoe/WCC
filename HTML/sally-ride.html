Sally Ride

I would like to be remembered as someone who was not afraid to do what she wanted to do, and as someone who took risks along the way in order to achieve her goals.

Sally Ride was the first woman in space. She was both an astronaut and physicist. Not only was she the first woman in space, she was also the youngest person to go into space. She was only 32 when she first entered outer space.

Interesting Facts

  1. She was a nationally ranked tennis player.
  2. She graduated from Stanford with a bachelor's degree in English and physics
  3. On June 18, 1983, she became the first woman to go into space
  4. She spent a total of 343 hours in space
